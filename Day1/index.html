FRONTLINES MEDIA(FLM)

FLM is a social media page and Edu Tech company that provides news, career guidance, and job opportunities:

News

FLM posts news in an entertaining way to help people stay informed about current affairs.

Career Guidance

FLM offers career guidance, mentorships, and job opportunities.

Student Community

FLM is the largest Telugu student community.

Content

FLM creates insightful content for students, freshers, and aspiring professionals.

Social Media

FLM is active on social media platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook.

Goal

FLM's goal is to help people learn about current affairs and society's issues, and to empower dreams and shape successful careers.
